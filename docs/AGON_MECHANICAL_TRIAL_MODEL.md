# Agon Mechanical Trial Model

## Model

A mechanical Agon trial is a bounded rehearsal for one pressure family before the live arena protocol exists.

It has a trigger boundary, prerequisites, participating agents, assistant support roles, allowed lawful moves, decision points, handoffs, fallback posture, expected evidence, expected artifacts, owner-review requests, and terminal pre-protocol outcomes.

## What mechanical means

Mechanical does not mean shallow. It means the trial tests the system's bones before testing high doctrine.

The first trial families are trace, fallback honesty, contradiction endurance, costly closure, assistant escalation, prediction discipline, and summon intent cost.

## No live protocol

Mechanical trial playbooks may prepare future arena input. They do not perform arena adjudication.
