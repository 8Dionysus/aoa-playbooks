# Agon Trial Owner Handoffs

Trial playbooks may name owner handoffs without absorbing neighboring authority.

`aoa-routing` may receive gate hint candidates.

`aoa-evals` may receive proof and breach review requests.

`aoa-memo` may receive bounded chronicle candidates later, but not durable scars from playbook authority.

`aoa-stats` may receive non-sovereign summary requests.

`aoa-skills` may later implement bounded workflows named by the route.

`aoa-techniques` may later refine reusable practices behind lawful moves.

## Stop-line

If a playbook starts deciding outcome legitimacy, it is becoming eval or arena protocol. Stop.
