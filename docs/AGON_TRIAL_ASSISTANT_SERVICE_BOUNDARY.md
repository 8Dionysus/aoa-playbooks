# Agon Trial Assistant Service Boundary

Assistants can serve trial playbooks by making the route legible.

They may prepare receipts, preserve input/output shape, flag scope breach, request missing fields, hand off to an agonic actor, and package owner-review tickets.

They may not gain:

- contestant_seat
- judge_seat
- closer_jurisdiction
- summon_initiator_jurisdiction
- scar_writer
- rank_mutator
- tos_promoter

The legal formula remains:

```text
Assistants may help the trial become legible. Assistants may not become hidden contestants.
```
